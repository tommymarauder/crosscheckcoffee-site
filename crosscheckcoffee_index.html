Our Story

Born from the heart of a veteran and rink rat, CrossCheck Coffee is more than just a brand—it’s a legacy forged in grit, sacrifice, and the sweat of the ice. Founded by Tommy Barron, a combat-tested infantryman and lifelong hockey enthusiast, we’re committed to fueling those who chase their passions with relentless dedication. From the roar of the crowd to the hush of pre-dawn pucks on ice, we know what it’s like to grind. Every bag of coffee we roast is a tribute to the grinders—the vets, the dads, the grinders who still live and bleed hockey. We’re not in this to get rich. We’re in this to give back.

Our mission: Serve up bold, unapologetic coffee that keeps you powered through the toughest shifts, the longest practices, and life’s biggest battles.
